## Additional Style Notes for All Images:

1. **Consistency**: All images should have the same professional, clean aesthetic
2. **Color Harmony**: Stick to blue (#3b82f6, #2563eb) and white as primary colors
3. **Lighting**: Bright, clean, professional lighting throughout
4. **Composition**: Wide shots that show scale and detail
5. **Quality**: High resolution, realistic, professional photography style
6. **No People**: Keep images focused on facilities and equipment (except the "who we are" image)
7. **Regulatory Focus**: Emphasize cleanliness, precision, and compliance in all images
8. **Modern Aesthetic**: Contemporary pharmaceutical facility design with advanced technology
